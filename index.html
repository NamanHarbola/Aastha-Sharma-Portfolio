Introduction

Hi, I'm
Aastha 👋

An artist and craft enthusiast passionate about creating meaningful, hands-on creative experiences.

I work across a variety of mediums including pipe cleaner crafts, crochet, embroidery, clay modelling, sketching, and painting. Through community workshops, I've discovered how rewarding it is to help people learn new skills, express themselves creatively, and connect with others.

"Art doesn't have to be perfect, it just has to be yours."

Philosophy
🌟

Creativity for Everyone

Art should feel accessible regardless of experience. There's no wrong way to create.

🤝

Community First

Workshops are a space to connect and create together — the community is the magic.

🙌

Learn by Doing

The best way to learn is through hands-on exploration. Jump in and see what happens.

💫

Process Over Perfection

The joy of creating matters more than the final result. Embrace the mess!
